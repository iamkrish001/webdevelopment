My Favourite Food: Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs (700g) chicken pieces (bone-in for more flavor)
  • 2 tablespoons oil (vegetable or canola)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated or minced
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 cup coconut milk (optional for a creamy texture)
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped cilantro leaves for garnish

Receipe

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Wash the chicken pieces and pat them dry. If using bone-in pieces, they add more flavor to the curry.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent. Then add minced garlic and grated ginger, stirring for a couple of minutes until fragrant.

  3. Add Spices: Stir in the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften. Add turmeric powder, ground coriander, ground cumin, chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook the spices for a few minutes until the oil starts to separate from the mixture.

  4. Cook the Chicken: Add the chicken pieces to the spice mixture, ensuring they are well coated. Cook for a few minutes until the chicken starts to brown slightly.

  5. Simmer: Pour in water (or chicken broth) enough to cover the chicken pieces. Bring it to a gentle boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let it simmer for about 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender. If using coconut milk for a creamy texture, add it during this step.

  6. Adjust Seasoning: Taste and adjust the seasoning according to your preference. If the curry is too thick, add a little water to reach your desired consistency.

  7. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle garam masala over the curry and garnish with chopped cilantro leaves before serving. Serve hot with rice or naan bread.
